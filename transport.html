Policy & Safety

Important Information

Timings

Morning pick-up: 6:20 AM – 7:30 AM depending on route. Afternoon drop: 2:00 PM – 3:30 PM. Exact times shared on registration.

GPS Tracking App

Parents can track their child's bus/van in real-time via our school app. Alerts sent on arrival & departure from school.

Safety Protocols

All drivers undergo police verification, annual medical checkups, and defensive driving training. Zero tolerance for rash driving.

Fee Payment

Fees are collected quarterly. Accepted via cheque, NEFT/RTGS, or online school portal. No cash accepted at the counter.

Cancellation Policy

One month's written notice required for cancellation. Refund of remaining quarter fees (after deducting ₹1000 admin fee) processed within 15 days.
